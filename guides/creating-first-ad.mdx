# Creating Your First Ad

This guide walks you through creating and displaying your first advertisement in PromoMaster Pro, from initial setup to seeing your content on screen.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Management Panel">
    Start by navigating to the **Gestión Total** (Management) section from the sidebar. This is where you'll create and configure all your content.

    The management panel has three tabs:
    - **Productos** - For product advertisements
    - **Avisos Libres** - For custom announcements
    - **Sucursales** - For store locations
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Store Location">
    Before creating ads, you need at least one store location configured.

    1. Click the **Sucursales** tab
    2. Fill in the store form:
       - **Nombre**: Your store name (e.g., "Sucursal Central")
       - **Dirección**: Store address (e.g., "Av. Libertador 1234")
       - **Logo Color**: Select a brand color for your store
    3. Click **Guardar Local** to save

    <Note>
      PromoMaster Pro comes with a default store ("Sucursal Central") already configured. You can edit or create additional stores as needed.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Product">
    Now let's create a product advertisement.

    1. Click the **Productos** tab
    2. In the "Nuevo Producto" form on the left, fill in:
       - **Nombre**: Product name (e.g., "Carne Premium")
       - **Precio**: Price in your currency (e.g., 5990)
       - **Unidad**: Select unit type (unidad, kg, g, litro, pack)
       - **Categoría**: Product category (e.g., "Carnes")

    <Info>
      Leave optional fields empty for now. You can add images and slogans later.
    </Info>

    3. Click **Crear Producto** to save

    Your product will appear in the list on the right side of the screen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Product Details (Optional)">
    Enhance your product ad with visual elements:

    ### Adding an Image
    
    PromoMaster Pro offers three ways to add product images:

    **URL Method**
    - Select the **URL** tab under "Imagen del Producto"
    - Paste the direct URL to your product image
    - The image will preview once loaded

    **Upload Method**
    - Select the **Subir** tab
    - Click **Seleccionar Archivo**
    - Choose an image from your computer
    - The image will be embedded in base64 format

    **AI Generation**
    - Select the **AI** tab
    - Ensure your product name is filled in
    - Click **Generar con IA**
    - Wait for Gemini AI to generate a professional product image

    ### Adding a Slogan
    
    - Manually type a slogan in the "Slogan Publicitario" field, OR
    - Click the sparkle icon (✨) to generate one with AI
    - The AI will create a compelling marketing slogan based on your product name and description

    ### Setting as Promotion
    
    To mark the product as a special offer:
    1. Check the **ES UNA OFERTA** checkbox
    2. Select the promotion type:
       - **Oferta Simple** - General promotion
       - **Oferta del Día** - Daily special
       - **Oferta Semanal** - Weekly deal
  </Step>

  <Step title="Build Your Playlist">
    Switch to the **Panel de Control** (Dashboard) to create your display sequence.

    1. You'll see three tabs at the top:
       - **Productos** - Browse your product catalog
       - **Avisos Libres** - Browse custom announcements
       - **Mi Secuencia** - Your playlist queue

    2. From the **Productos** tab:
       - Browse your products in a card grid layout
       - Click the **+** button on any product card to add it to your sequence
       - Or hover and click the play icon to preview it immediately

    3. Check your playlist:
       - Click the **Mi Secuencia** tab (shows a badge with item count)
       - Your added products appear in a numbered list
       - Use the up/down arrows to reorder items
       - Click the X icon to remove items

    <Tip>
      Click "Añadir Todo a Secuencia" to quickly add all products and announcements to your playlist.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Display Settings">
    Before launching, configure your display preferences:

    **Display Duration**
    - At the top of the Dashboard, find the duration control
    - Adjust the seconds value (default: 5 seconds)
    - This determines how long each item stays on screen

    **Select Store Location**
    - Use the "Local" dropdown at the top right
    - Select which store location to display
    - The store name and address will appear in your ads
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch Your Display">
    Ready to see your ad in action!

    1. From the **Mi Secuencia** tab, click the **Iniciar Transmisión** button
    2. Your display will launch in fullscreen mode with:
       - Smooth transitions between items
       - Dynamic background effects matching your brand colors
       - An animated ticker at the bottom
       - Progress bar at the top showing slide timing
       - Store information prominently displayed

    **Display Features**:
    - **Auto-advance**: Items rotate automatically based on your duration setting
    - **Progress indicator**: Top bar shows time remaining
    - **Slide dots**: Bottom center shows position in sequence
    - **Exit button**: Red X in the top right returns to Dashboard

    <Warning>
      The display runs in fullscreen mode. Press the red X button or press ESC to exit.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Quick Preview Option">
    Want to preview a single item without building a playlist?

    From the **Productos** or **Avisos Libres** tab:
    1. Hover over any product or announcement card
    2. Click the play icon (▶) that appears
    3. The item displays immediately in fullscreen
    4. Click the exit button when done

    This is perfect for testing individual items before adding them to your main sequence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My product doesn't appear in the Dashboard">
    Make sure you clicked "Crear Producto" after filling in the form. Check the product list on the right side of the Management panel to confirm it was saved.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Display shows blank screen">
    This happens when:
    - Your playlist is empty (add items first)
    - The selected store doesn't exist (check store selection dropdown)
    - Items were deleted after being added to playlist (rebuild your sequence)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="AI features not working">
    AI features (image generation, slogan generation) require:
    - A valid API key configured in your environment
    - Active internet connection
    - Gemini API access enabled
    
    Check your `API_KEY` environment variable if AI features fail.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
