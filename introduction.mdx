## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Multi-Store Management">
    Manage multiple store locations with customizable branding. Each store can have its own name, address, and brand colors for consistent visual identity across your signage network.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Content Generation">
    Leverage Google Gemini's latest models to automatically generate professional product images and compelling marketing slogans. Save time and maintain high-quality creative content.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mix Media Playlists">
    Create custom sequences mixing products and announcements with drag-and-drop ordering. Control exactly what appears on your screens and when.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Premium Display Experience">
    Full-screen visualization optimized for tablets and computers with smooth animations, dynamic lighting effects, and impactful typography that captures attention.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Local Storage Persistence">
    All changes are automatically saved to local storage, allowing you to work offline without losing any data when you refresh or close the application.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Responsive Design">
    Adapted for perfect experience on both management dashboard and full-screen exhibition mode across different screen sizes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Perfect For

- **Retail Stores**: Showcase products with dynamic pricing and promotional displays
- **Restaurants**: Display menu items with appetizing imagery and daily specials
- **Corporate Offices**: Share announcements, company information, and branded content
- **Event Spaces**: Manage multiple screens with coordinated messaging
- **Shopping Centers**: Coordinate advertising across multiple tenant locations

## Technology Stack

PromoMaster Pro is built with modern web technologies for performance and reliability:

- **React 19**: Latest version for optimal UI performance
- **TypeScript**: Type-safe development
- **Vite**: Lightning-fast build tool
- **Google Gemini API**: AI-powered content generation
- **Lucide React**: Beautiful, consistent icons
- **Local Storage**: Client-side data persistence
